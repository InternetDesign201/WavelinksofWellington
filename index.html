Surveillance Cameras

Surveillance camera's are a video camera that records people's activities in order to detect and prevent crime.

Dome Camera

Dome cameras tend to have a wider viewing angle, and are often more discreet than other surveillance cameras

Bullet Camera

Bullet cameras are named for their long cylindrical shape, which vaguely resembles a bullet. The distinctive appearance is highly recognizable providing a visible deterrent to intruders.

Wires

There are various wires that exist to help the interent work as it should do

Fiber Optic Cable

Fiber optic cabling consists of a center glass core surrounded by several layers of protective materials

Ethernet Cable and Connector

There are different methods that can be used for shielding Ethernet cables. The most common is to place a shield around each twisted pair. This not only provides shielding for the cable externally, but also reduces crosstalk between the internal twisted pairs as well.

Coaxil Cable

Coaxial cables form a transmission line and confine the electromagnetic wave inside the cable between the center conductor and the shield.

Modems

There are two different types of modem: Ethernet modems that plug into the network card in the computer, and wireless modems that connect to a computer using a wireless LAN (WLAN).
